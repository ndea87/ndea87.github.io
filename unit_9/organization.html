Organization Survey

Ask yourself these questions on a daily basis to ensure you've completed and organized the tasks you need to do. Record all of your answers in a word document.

  • What essential tasks do you need to get done today?
  • What nonessential tasks do you need to get done today?
  • List off your essential tasks and take notes of the directions you need to follow to get the tasks done.
  • Set deadlines for each of your tasks.
  • Do any of your tasks involve the help of another co-worker or co-workers? Mark the tasks that need the help of other co-workers with an "H".
  • List off the tasks you've completed as you complete them. Is the task essential or nonessential?
  • Have you checked and responded to all of your emails? Check to see if all emails that needed a reply have a "reply" sign. Alternatively, you could send all emails you've replied to into a separate folder.
  • Have you checked your phone for voice messages?

After 4 weeks, email all of your answers to noora.abdulkerim@gmail.com to receive a concise report on your efficiency and progress at work. The report will also include areas that you need to strengthen to become more efficient at work.
