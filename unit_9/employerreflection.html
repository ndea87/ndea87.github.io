Self-reflection assessment

Reflection Questionnaire

Ask yourself these questions on a weekly basis to ensure you're monitoring both your professional, personal and emotional growth. Please note that this is the same reflection questionnaire as the one in the "For Employees" section only with a changed perspective. Examine the answers to your questions on a monthly basis and check to see if there are any trends in your answers. Are the trends positive or negative? Explain.

  • On a scale of 1 (very poor) to 10 (excellent), how would you rate your productivity? Why?
  • On a scale of 1 (very poor) to 10 (excellent), how would you rate how overwhelmed you felt? Why?
  • On a scale of 1 (low) to 10 (high), rate how happy you felt at work. Why did you give yourself this rating.
  • On a scale of 1(low) to 10 (high), rate how anxious you felt at work this week. Why did you give yourself this rating? Did your anxiety come from the same source or did it come from different sources?
  • How accurate was the results of your work this week? Did you have a large margin for error? Why or why not?
  • How would you describe your progress in terms of relationship building with your employees?
  • How focused were you this week on your tasks? Did you feel more distracted than usual? Why or why not?
  • How would you describe your progress in terms of networking? Professional Development (conference, independent research on new strategies or relevant content, informational interviews)?
  • How would you describe your value as an asset to your employees this week? Did your management this week make your employees especially productive or did you feel like your management was subpar? Why or why not?
  • At this point, what do you feel like you can do improve yourself as an employer?
