Commmunication evaluation

The purpose of the evaluation below is to informally assess the status of your communication with your employee. Please note that this evaluation is the same evaluation that is in the "for employee" section of the website but only with a changed perspective. Try to conduct this evaluation on a weekly basis. Take note of your areas of weakness and your areas of strength.

  • Have you communicated with your employee this week? If so, for what purpose?
  • Which means did you use to communicate with your employee? Face to face? Email? Other?
  • How would you describe the tone of the way you communicated with your employee? Professional and firm? Informal? Insecure? Unsure? Tense? Anxious? Jovial? Other?
  • How would you describe of your employee's response? Professional? Informal? Agitated? Abrupt? Frank? Fearful? Unclear? Other?
  • Do you feel like the way you communicate with your employee is effective? Does you welcome opportunities for your employee to communicate with you or are you hesistant to do this? Why?
  • On a scale of 1 to 10, 1 being very poor and 10 being excellent, how would you rate your communication with your employee? Are you satisfied with this rating? Why or why not?
  • How do you feel like you could improve your communication with your employee? What could you change to make your communication more effective? Why do you feel like this would work?
  • Do you feel like you need to change anything to be a more effective communicator? What should be changed? How?
