Commmunication evaluation

The purpose of the evaluation below is to informally assess the status of your communication with your employer. Try to conduct this evaluation on a weekly basis. Take note of your areas of weakness and your areas of strength.

  • Have you communicated with your employer this week? If so, for what purpose?
  • Which means did you use to communicate with your employer? Face to face? Email? Other?
  • How would you describe the tone of the way you communicated with your employer? Professional and firm? Informal? Insecure? Unsure? Tense? Anxious? Jovial? Other?
  • How would you describe of your employer's response? Professional? Informal? Agitated? Abrupt? Frank? Other?
  • Do you feel like the way you communicate with your employer is effective? Does your employer welcome opportunities for you to communicate with him or her or does your employer seem hesistant?
  • On a scale of 1 to 10, 1 being very poor and 10 being excellent, how would you rate your communication with your employer? Are you satisfied with this rating? Why or why not?
  • How do you feel like you could improve your communication with your employer? What could you change to make your communication more effective? Why do you feel like this would work?
  • Do you feel like your employer needs to change anything to be a more effective communicator? What should be changed? Is there a way for you to make that suggestion? How?
